Nature perfected the alarm, we open sourced it

A wireless alarm device purpose-built for life’s critical moments.

About Krake

Krake is a wireless open-source alarm device based on the Public Invention General Purpose Alarm Device.

Features

Multilingual audio, Wi‑Fi and MQTT, LED levels, rotary knob, flexible power, mute button, open-source licensing, and HL7 compatibility.

Who needs Krake?

Care at home, medical equipment, and IT infrastructure.

How it works

Condition detected. Krake receives it. You are alerted.
