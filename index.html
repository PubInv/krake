About Krake

Krake is an open-source wireless alarm device based on the Public Invention General Purpose Alarm Device.

The Name

The name Krake is based on the crake bird and its distinctive alarming call.

Everything an alarm should be

Features include multilingual audio, Wi-Fi, MQTT, LEDs, rotary control, mute, and open-source licensing.

Who needs Krake?

Krake supports care at home, medical equipment, and IT infrastructure alarm needs.

Simple by design

Condition detected, Krake receives it, and the human is alerted.

The Team

The Krake team includes invention coaches and public inventors.
